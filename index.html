    A Honda Monkey custom project. The project was started in summer 2016 and were completed in fall 2016. It took about 4 months to complete project. Project started with a junked Honda Monkey to transform it to a showbike. Almost every part is changed out. The swing is +8cm longer with discbrakes, front fork is 510mm long with disc brakes, wheels are 10", custom remade cupseat and many chrome detailed parts. The paint was painted in Chameleon green color by a paintshop. The Honda Monkey attended a motorshow, Westcoast Motorshow 2017.
    Project video on YouTube here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ssoyljSIUv0

    An iceracing and drifting car project for fun started in late 2017. The car is completely stripped out and equipped with bucket seats, hydraulic e-brake, spike tires, coilovers and some other related things. The differential is welded and the ratio is changed to 3.64 (2.93 stock). Project is not finished yet.
